The Opportunity

Nigeria has mandated decarbonisation.
No platform exists to deliver it.

The NUPRC's Upstream Petroleum Decarbonisation Template (UPDT) became mandatory in January 2025. Every licence, permit, and FDP submission now requires a verified decarbonisation strategy. Yet 75% of Nigeria's 280+ licensed operators lack even the most basic GHG reporting infrastructure.
