Module 05 — AI / ML

Verde Intelligence
AI & ML Engine

Four machine learning models embedded directly into the platform workflow — not bolted on as an afterthought. Verde Intelligence reduces the "tenfold variance in methane estimates" documented by Bloomfield LP, forecasts your compliance risk, and optimises your decarbonisation pathway.

ML-1: Anomaly Detection — Isolation Forest
Real-time validation on every data entry. Flags statistically improbable readings (e.g., 400% spike in compressor CH₄ with no operational change logged). Confidence score + nearest 5 comparable facility-months for context.
ML-2: Emissions Forecasting — Prophet + LSTM
24-month facility-level emissions forecast with 80%/95% prediction intervals. Powers the UPDT target-setting wizard — shows whether proposed reduction targets are achievable under current operations.
ML-3: Compliance Risk Scoring — XGBoost
Probability (0–100) that an operator will breach a NUPRC deadline within 30/60/90 days. Score ≥65 triggers CSM alert. Score ≥80 triggers NUPRC regulator flag in partner portal.
ML-4: Decarbonisation Pathway Optimiser — NSGA-II
Multi-objective optimisation across max CO₂e reduction, min CAPEX, min implementation time. Generates three Pareto-optimal scenarios with Gantt roadmaps and carbon credit revenue estimates per scenario.
