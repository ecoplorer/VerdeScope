We don't do generic platform walkthroughs. Book a VerdeScope demo and we'll run a live UPDT compliance gap analysis on your actual OML/OPL/PPL profile, calculate your current Scope 1 emissions estimate using IPCC Tier 1 factors, and project your potential carbon revenue — all before you sign anything.

What happens in your demo
01
UPDT Compliance Gap Analysis
We map your licence type, concession, and operating profile against all five UPDT obligations and generate a live Compliance Readiness Score. You see exactly what's missing and what it will cost to not fix it.
02
Live Emissions Estimate
Using your production volumes and facility type, we run an IPCC Tier 1 emissions calculation live on screen — gas flaring, fugitive methane, combustion, pneumatics — and show your estimated annual CO₂e inventory.
03
Carbon Revenue Projection
We show the carbon credit pipeline your emissions data unlocks under Nigeria's Carbon Market Framework — using VCS VM0039 methodology and current VCM price data. Most operators are surprised by the number.
04
Platform Walkthrough
Full live tour of the LDAR Manager, Equipment Tracker, UPDT Workflow Engine, and Verde Intelligence AI — configured to your operator profile so every screen is relevant to your specific facilities.
